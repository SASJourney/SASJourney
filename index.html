Welcome to SAS

SAS is a congress in the space of sciences and arts, forecast for the year 2075. Our goal is for artists of all kinds to collaborate with the brilliant minds of modern science, to mobilize young persons from around the world to be part of a space congress.

It is worth mentioning that SAS is going to work together with the space program, Open Space, to mobilize the Latin American population, which has a lot of potential but little access to space programs.



Get in touch with us!

If you think you have what it takes to be part of this group of artists and scientists, do not hesitate to send us your request. On the other hand, if you have any questions about the event, don't hesitate to ask us!
